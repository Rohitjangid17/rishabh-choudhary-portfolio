What are National Games ?

The National Games of India are like the country's own Olympics, where athletes from different states compete in various sports.

They started in the 1920s as the Indian Olympic Games and later became the National Games. The goal is to promote sports and find new talent. The games include many sports like athletics, archery, badminton, boxing, and more.

They are a big deal in India and help the country's sports grow. The games also face challenges, but they are an important part of India's sports history and help young athletes.
