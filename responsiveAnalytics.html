Attrition

Attrition refers to the gradual reduction of a company's workforce when employees leave and are not replaced.

It can occur for variety of reasons:

  • Resignation
  • Retirement
  • Death
  • Termination
  • Elimination of position
  • Other Life Events

Attrition is often seen as a normal part of a company's workforce dynamics. It can be caused by employees transitioning to new phases of their lives or careers.
